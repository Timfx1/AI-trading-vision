This project originally had:

- backend/dataset/valid_setup/ : ~X images from TradingView (valid setups)
- backend/dataset/invalid_setup/ : ~Y images from TradingView (invalid setups)
- backend/outputs/model.keras : trained CNN model
- backend/data/image_index.pkl + embedding_index.pkl : similarity search indexes

These are NOT included in the ZIP to keep it small.
To recreate:
1. Place your screenshots into backend/dataset/valid_setup and invalid_setup.
2. Run: py backend/scripts/train_cnn_model.py
3. Run: py backend/scripts/build_image_index.py
4. Run: py backend/scripts/build_embedding_index.py
